Hey! I am Archana Vaidheeswaran

As a Program Director at Algoverse, I oversee AI Research programs and help grow global AI safety initiatives through fellowships and community-building. I also serve on the Board of Directors at Women in Machine Learning.

I run PRISM (Peer-vetted Research Initiative for Safety Methodologies) under Women Who Do Data — a 16-week AI safety research program that uses peer selection to scale mentorship, turning the senior-researcher bottleneck into a multiplier. Our EleutherAI SOAR pilot produced two accepted papers (NeurIPS 2025, ICASSP 2025) at a 25:1 researcher-to-mentor ratio.

When I'm not thinking about AI, you'll probably find me cheering on indie game creators as a Community Director at International Game Developers Association (IGDA).
